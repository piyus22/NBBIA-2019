About the Workshop

Bioinformatics emerged in early 1970s which was the amalgamation of the Biology, Computer Science, Information Technology, Statistics, Mathematics, and several principles from various other areas.Bioinformatics is the interdisciplinary field of science that uses the associated fields to analyze and interpret biological data.The high usage of information technology in the field of biology increased the career prospects in this field. Use of bioinformatics tools in life sciences has become necessary to analyze experimental data since omics have emerged in this field. The large amount of data is a challenge for the biological and biomedical community. Biomedical signal and image processing is the utmost requirement in current healthcare sectors. With the increase in several lifestyle disease health care is on priority to facilitate human beings through technological interventions. It is anticipated that this workshop will help the biologists, biotechnologists, biomedical scientists and students from all these streams to gain insight into biological and biomedical data analyses. It will also help engineers, mathematicians, statisticians, and computer scientists to explore new dimensions for their academic and research activities.

  Objectives of Workshop


  The objectives of the workshop are:

  •   To provide awareness about the requirement and scope of bioinformatics and biomedical data analysis.
  •   To introduce bioinformatics and biomedical dataset.
  •   To familiarize audience with basic tools used in bioinformatics for various applications.
  •   To provide hands-on practice on analysis of biomedical images using MATLAB.

      Workshop Info


      Bioinformatics is all about converting biological data into meaningful information. Broadly it covers

  •     Collecting data,
  •    Analyzing data, and
  •    Interpreting results in a meaningful way.

    • Bioinformatics is the application of computational techniques to solve problems in biology. In this workshop, we tend to focus on bioinformatics and health sciences.

    TOPICS TO BE COVERED

  • Introduction to Bioinformatics, objectives and applications
  • Bioinformatics resources, data extraction and analysis
  • Biological sequence alignments and analyses
  • Computational and functional Genomics
  • Homology Modeling
  • Molecular Docking
  • Offline and online Tools for Bioinformatics Analyses
  • Introduction to Image Processing using MATLAB
  • Introduction to Computer Aided Diagnosis System designed for the clinical practitioners
  • Acquisition of Medical Images, Signals and their processing
  • MATLAB tools for image enhancement and image segmentation
  • MATLAB tools for feature extraction from Medical Images
  • MATLAB tools for classification of abnormalities in Medical Images.
